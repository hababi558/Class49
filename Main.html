Interesting things About Camel.

Interesting things About chameleon.

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleon's
  • Most of Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting things About pigeons.

  • Pigeons are very complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowed for their navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing ability

Interesting things About Teddy bear.

  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
